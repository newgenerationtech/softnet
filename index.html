SOFTNET, a non-for Profit Organization, was conceptualized and established in 2019 by innovative and dedicated young Liberian I.T Professionals who believe that the computer industry plays a major role in positive shifting the future of our beloved country mama-Liberia through providing computer education to everyone in all sectors and aspect of life regardless of financial status, ethnic background, religious affiliation or political views. SOFTNET remains dedicated to providing an array of professional-hands-on computer knowledge and solutions to all.

Objectives

SOFTNET LIBERIA was established to help train potential computer users in Liberia to enhance the smooth operation in their various areas of work in all sectors such as in Health, Education, and Security; to discover their truth career path in the computer industry, through giving Liberian the foundational skills necessary for them to succeed in their work places, and increase their knowledge with computers, Networking and also to connect them to the right career path in the Computer Industry. Below are some core objectives:

  • create a conducive Computer learning environment for all Liberian
  • To make computer learning affordable for all Liberian
  • To create the mindset in each individual to model a real-world problem in Liberia into a computer based application for better solution that will improve our Liberian society
  • To create a suitable and reliable research environment for all.

Mission

SofNet-Liberia, a non-for Profit Organization established in 2019, is dedicated to providing computer education and/or solutions to everyone in all sectors and aspect of life regardless of social or economic status, religious affiliation, political views or ethnic background. SoftNet Liberia Designs and promote the platform where all Liberian who desires to start a career in the field of Information Technology (I.T) or Information Community & Technology (ICT) can have an affordable access to learn computer or advanced on an existing computer knowledge and be able to solve real-world problems effecting their communities or society.

Back to top

Vision

SoftNet Liberia envisioned to:

  • See every Liberian become computer literate (to increase computer literacy rate in Liberia)
  • See students become competent researchers using computer base research methodology
  • See those who are computer literate become employ in various institutions of work that need competent I.T. employees
  • To see that 45 – 50% of various institutions’ operation be performed by computer users (computerize operations)
